Exposed: Best ways to uncover your God given purpose for your life and fulfil it
  • Admin
  • May 12, 2020
  • 10 Comments

“What am I meant to do with my life? What is my purpose? What am I here for?” Beyond the quarter or mid-life crisis monologue, you’ve likely asked yourself these questions over time and never really put in the effort to think about it. After coaching hundreds of bloggers and entrepreneurs in the last 3 years, I’ve finally figured out an equation that will help you find clarity in your life’s legacy. After all, legacy is what we’re after here. The goal isn't to live forever; the goal is to create something that will.CLICK TO TWEET And though you might have clarity on your purpose after this video, it doesn’t mean you immediately begin to fulfill it. It means you now have a direction to move towards. My business coach Brendon Burchard would always say, “randomness is the key to mediocrity” and once you gain clarity, you then develop a strategy. After the strategy, you begin to take action. Slow, but forward action. Because this journey is a long one, and the marathon of life is meant to be lived one day at a time. While I’ve been blogging for 19 years, traveling for 7, and living for 30, there are so many paths I’ve been on, and it was hard to figure out which road I was meant to take to ultimately lead to becoming the best version of myself. Sometimes you might take detours, life might hit you with some dead ends, but you never quit. The character is built on the journey, not at the destination. And you need to learn to love the process just as much as the result. Enjoy the video and feel free to share with a friend who’s also on a path of self-discovery.
